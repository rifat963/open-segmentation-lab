About Open Segmentation Lab

This project is a static, free academic website for learning image segmentation through tutorials, references, formulas, paper summaries, and small browser-based tools.

Purpose of the Platform

Open Segmentation Lab is intended to help students understand the major segmentation tasks before they implement or evaluate models. The site emphasizes correct terminology, readable explanations, and practical academic context.

Intended Users

The primary users are university students, instructors, early-stage computer vision researchers, and project teams who need a clear starting point for segmentation concepts and evaluation.

Free for Students and Researchers

All educational content is free to read and share. The site has no login system, no paid course gate, no backend, and no database requirement.

Advertising Policy

Limited advertisements may appear only to support free hosting and maintenance. Ads should remain clearly labeled, sparse, and separated from formulas, code blocks, interactive tools, navigation buttons, and learning-critical content.

Academic promise: Open Segmentation Lab should remain a learning resource first. Any future sponsorship or ad placement must not make the site feel commercial or reduce access to educational material.
