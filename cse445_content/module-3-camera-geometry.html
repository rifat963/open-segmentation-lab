Infographic

The camera geometry chain.

W

World point

A 3D point exists in a world coordinate frame.

E

Extrinsics

Rotation and translation place the point in camera coordinates.

K

Intrinsics

Focal length, principal point, and skew map camera coordinates to pixels.

I

Image point

The final measurement appears as a 2D pixel location.
