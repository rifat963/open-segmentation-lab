Module 2

Feature Detection and Description

Study how stable interest points and local descriptors make image matching possible under scale, rotation, illumination, and viewpoint changes.

Lecture Focus

Mapped to CO1 with quiz emphasis in the course outline.

Lecture 3: Corners, Interest Points and Harris Detector

Repeatability, distinctiveness, edges versus corners, structure tensor, Harris response, thresholding, and non-maximum suppression.

Lecture 4: FAST, SIFT and SURF

FAST segment tests, SIFT scale-space extrema and descriptors, Gaussian pyramids, DoG, SURF integral images, and Hessian approximation.

Lecture 5: ORB and Local Descriptor Robustness

ORB as oFAST plus rBRIEF, binary descriptors, Hamming distance, invariance properties, benchmarking, and detector selection.
