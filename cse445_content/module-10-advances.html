Module 10

Advances in Computer Vision

Survey modern application domains and frontier ideas across autonomous driving, medical vision, robotics, surveillance, agriculture, vision-language models, and 3D generation.

Lecture Focus

Mapped to CO3 and course review activities from the course outline.

Lecture 22: Autonomous Driving Perception

Lane detection, pedestrian detection, traffic sign recognition, sensor fusion, PointPillars overview, KITTI, nuScenes, and Waymo datasets.

Lecture 23: Robotics Vision and Surveillance Analytics

Visual odometry, ORB-SLAM3, 6-DOF pose estimation, crowd density, person re-identification, privacy, and ethics.

Lecture 24: Course Review and Emerging Frontiers

Concept map from features to geometry to motion to recognition to tracking, plus ViT, SAM, CLIP, diffusion, NeRF, Gaussian splatting, and event cameras.
