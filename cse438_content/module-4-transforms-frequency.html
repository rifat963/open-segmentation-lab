Module 4

Transforms, Compression and Frequency Domain Filtering

Represent images by frequency content and transform coefficients to filter, compress, reconstruct, and detect structures such as lines and circles.

Lecture Focus

Mapped to CO2 from the course outline.

Lecture 17: Image Transforms and DFT

2D DFT, magnitude spectrum, phase spectrum, centering, and interpreting low versus high frequency components.

Lecture 18: Image Compression, DCT and Wavelets

DCT coefficients, block artifacts, transform coding, Haar and Daubechies wavelet decomposition, and reconstruction.

Lectures 19-20: Hough Transform and Frequency Filters

Line and circle detection using Hough space plus ideal, Butterworth, and Gaussian low-pass and high-pass filters.

Satellite stripe noise

A remote sensing image has periodic stripe noise. Explain why frequency analysis can expose the pattern more clearly than a spatial filter.

A dashcam image contains long lane markings. Compare Hough transform detection with edge maps alone.
