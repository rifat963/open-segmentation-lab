Scenario Driven Exploration

Counting blood cells

Two cells touch after thresholding. Decide which morphological operations can separate or clean the regions before connected-component analysis.

PCB defect inspection

A circuit board mask has tiny holes and broken traces. Explain how opening, closing, top-hat, and black-hat transforms reveal different defect types.
