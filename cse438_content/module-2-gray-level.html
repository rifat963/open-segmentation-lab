Scenario Driven Exploration

Night surveillance frame

A low-light image hides a doorway. Compare log transformation, gamma correction, and histogram equalization for revealing details without over-amplifying noise.

Remote sensing comparison

Two satellite images were captured in different seasons. Explain how histogram matching can make visual comparison fairer, and when it might mislead analysis.
